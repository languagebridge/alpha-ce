## What is LanguageBridge?

**LanguageBridge** is a Chrome browser extension that provides real-time translation and text-to-speech services to help students understand academic content in their native language. The tool supports 9 languages:

- Spanish
- Arabic
- Persian (Farsi)
- Dari
- Pashto
- Ukrainian
- Urdu
- Somali
- English

## How Does LanguageBridge Work?

When installed on a student's device (Chromebook, laptop, or desktop), LanguageBridge allows students to:

1. **Select text** on any webpage or Google Doc
2. **Click translate** to see the text in their preferred language
3. **Hear the translation** read aloud using text-to-speech
4. **Look up academic vocabulary** in plain English
5. **Use "Talk to Teacher"** feature for two-way voice translation

The tool is designed to help English learners access grade-level content while they continue developing English proficiency.

## Student Privacy & Ohio SB 29 Compliance

[School District Name] prioritizes student privacy and data security. LanguageBridge is **fully compliant with Ohio Senate Bill 29**, which establishes strict requirements for technology providers working with K-12 schools.

### What Data Does LanguageBridge Collect?

**LanguageBridge collects MINIMAL data:**

✅ **Anonymous usage statistics** (number of translations, languages used)
✅ **Technical error logs** (to fix bugs and improve service)

**LanguageBridge does NOT collect:**

❌ Student names or identifiers
❌ Student ID numbers
❌ Email addresses
❌ The actual text that students translate
❌ Browsing history
❌ Location data
❌ Keystroke patterns
❌ Screen recordings

### How is Student Data Protected?

- **No Educational Records Stored** - Translations are processed in real-time and immediately discarded. No student work is saved.
- **Enterprise-Grade Security** - Uses Microsoft Azure (FERPA-compliant) for translation processing
- **HTTPS Encryption** - All data transmitted securely
- **No Data Selling** - Student data is NEVER sold or shared with third parties
- **No Advertising** - The extension contains no advertisements or tracking
- **FERPA & COPPA Compliant** - Meets federal student privacy standards

### Monitoring Limitations (Per SB 29)

LanguageBridge does NOT monitor:
- Student location
- Keystroke activity
- Web browsing history
- Screen content (except text students manually select for translation)

Audio features (text-to-speech, speech-to-text) only activate when students click the microphone or speaker buttons. No passive monitoring or recording occurs.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is LanguageBridge required for my child?**
A: No. LanguageBridge is an optional support tool. Students can opt out without penalty.

**Q: Will using LanguageBridge affect my child's grades?**
A: No. LanguageBridge is a learning support tool to help students understand content. Teachers assess student learning through assignments and assessments, not tool usage.

**Q: Can teachers see what my child is translating?**
A: No. LanguageBridge does not provide individual student tracking. Teachers cannot see what text students translate.

**Q: Does my child need to create an account?**
A: No. LanguageBridge works immediately without requiring a login or account creation.

**Q: What if I have concerns about my child's privacy?**
A: Please contact [District Privacy Officer Name] at [Email/Phone]. We take student privacy seriously and are happy to address any concerns.

**Q: Can my child use LanguageBridge at home?**
A: Yes, if installed on a district-issued device. The extension works on any website, including homework and research sites.

**Q: Is there a cost to families?**
A: No. LanguageBridge is provided at no cost to students and families. [School District Name] covers the service costs.
